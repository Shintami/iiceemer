Venue Information

Venue, The Grand Hotel Taipei

The Grand Hotel Taipei is a historical five-star hotel, standing midway up Yuanshan, nestled against Yangmingshan, overlooking Keelung River in the front, Songshan in the east and Danshui in the west. The 14-storey palace-like building, with its vermilion columns, gilded tiles and a magnificent, grandiose, regal and classic presence, is an embodiment of eastern aesthetics and one of Taipei's representative landmarks. The hotel has 500 guest rooms, 4 Western and Chinese gourmet restaurants, and large banquet halls offering a wide range of different luxurious cuisines. Located in the city’s vantage point, Yuanshan, the Grand Hotel offers a great view of the prosperous city by day and an all-enveloping serenity at night. Over here, time and space expands and becomes one, while the classic meshes with the contemporary. The Grand Hotel Taipei's professional meeting planners are on hand to expertly tailor arrangements to suit the occasion and ensure a successful and memorable experience, throughout the event.
